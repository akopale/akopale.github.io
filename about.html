Who am I?

Hi there! I am Amy. I make maps, write words, and research relevant issues at the intersection of people and the environment. I have an undergraduate degree in geography with a minor in political science, and I am working on a master's degree in geography. I have completed advanced coursework in GIS, remote sensing, Lidar, and environmental geography.


When I am not studying, teaching undergraduates, or working on GIS projects, you can find me playing water polo at the Rec Center or taking in a concert with friends.
